Meal Kits

Healthy KITchen meal kits are assembled by our team and donated to food-insecure households with the help of our local food pantry partners. Each kit contains individually packaged ingredients that make up four complete meals.

Some of our pantry partners include:

  • Acton Food Pantry
  • Westford Food Pantry
  • Preventive Food Pantry at Boston Medical Center
  • Good Shepherd Lutheran Church
  • River Food Pantry
