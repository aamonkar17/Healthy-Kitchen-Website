Our Mission

Our mission at Healthy KITchen is to combat food insecurity—which has increased tremendously during the COVID-19 pandemic—by providing easy-to-make, nutritious, and tasty meals to our local communities.

Who We Serve

Healthy KITchen partners with local food pantries to provide healthy, nutritious meal kits to food-insecure households. 


Areas of service include:

  • Dane County, WI
  • Middlesex County, MA
  • Norfolk County, MA
  • Suffolk County, MA
